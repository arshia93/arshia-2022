Overview

Trym offered men the ability to book in home haircuts before, during, or after the barber shop's operating hours. We managed {%} conversion rate on all visitors, but with thousands of men looking a quality haircut, we had to improve our conversion rate to better scale. ¬†

Improving our conversion rate would improve our unit economics and allow us to reach thousands of more users. Our funnel was resulting in high customer acquisition costs. Furthermore as we scaled it was important to maintain a 3:1¬†LTV/CAC ratio.

If we were able to improve our funnel, then we'd also be able to improve the earnings for our barbers and get closer to reaching network effects.

I worked with our team of engineers and designers to isolate the source of the problem and to fill in the holes with either a new or improved funnel.

The objective was to:
- Increase booked appointments
- Increase the conversion rater per user
- Reduce customer acquisition costs

Problems and Solutions

Problem Solution(s)
Transparency Messaging at checkout
At checkout we can improve our messaging prior to payment by letting our users know that they will not be charged yet until the barber accepts their request and what they do after to prepare. This may ease concerns regarding booking and payment reducing drop offs at checkout.

Availability vs Date
Rather than selecting one specific time for a haircut, users can share their availability window. Theoretically, this increases our ability to match requests successfully while offering a more reasonable selection to both clients and barbers.

Improved landing page
We can redesign our landing page to quickly resonate with the concerns we‚Äôve uncovered with the team. The new landing page can address the main concerns throughout the customer journey: finding a barber, receiving the haircut at home, the cleanup, and rebooking.
Personalization Barber-to-client messaging
In order to improve transparency and reduce the personalization of our booking process, we can allow clients to message barbers. This allows them to discuss their hair, availability etc.

Matching based on hairstyle
During our interviews we‚Äôve noticed that client want a barber experienced in their hairstyle. We can set up a match making feature to match clients with barbers based on their inputed preferences and pictures. Only a barber that has reviewed the client‚Äôs desired haircut would be matched.

Selection based on hairstyle
A Yelp like selection that allows clients to pick their barber based on his/her profile. The profile can include pictures, descriptions, and reviews.
Rebooking New rebooking mechanism
After a user has completed his first haircut, he can return to our website and select to rebook without the need to go through their menu or booking process. We can provide a button to rebook with their favorite barber anywhere on our website.

Barber profiles
Rather than visiting our landing page users could directly rebook through our barber‚Äôs unique profile.
